Welcome to YEPIE Bot

YEPIE is an advanced and free Discord bot packed with useful features and easy-to-use commands. Whether you want leveling systems, moderation, giveaways, or invite tracking, YEPIE has you covered. Join thousands of servers and enhance your Discord experience today!

Commands

**MODERATION**
> • COMMAND: `ban` — USAGE: `?ban [user] [reason]`
> • COMMAND: `unban` — USAGE: `?unban [user] [case]`
> • COMMAND: `kick` — USAGE: `?kick [user] [reason]`
> • COMMAND: `mute` — USAGE: `?mute [user] [reason]`
> • COMMAND: `unmute` — USAGE: `?unmute [user] [case]`
> • COMMAND: `warn` — USAGE: `?warn [user] [reason]`
> • COMMAND: `unwarn` — USAGE: `?unwarn [user] [case]`

**LEVELING**
> • COMMAND: `leveling setup` — USAGE: `?leveling setup`
> • COMMAND: `leveling channel` — USAGE: `?leveling channel [channel]`
> • COMMAND: `leveling message` — USAGE: `?leveling message [message]`

**LOGS**

**WELCOME**

**GENERAL**
> • COMMAND: `role info` — USAGE: `?role info [role]`
